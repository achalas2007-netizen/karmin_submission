The Problem

AWS cloud spend is broken by design.

AWS cloud spending is notoriously difficult to manage. Teams spin up EC2 RDS EBS resources and forget about them. Reserved Instances go unpurchased, and right-sizing recommendations for Lambda S3 CloudFront pile up unactioned. Finance teams lack the technical context to optimize, and engineering teams lack the time. The result? $32 billion in cloud waste — every year.
